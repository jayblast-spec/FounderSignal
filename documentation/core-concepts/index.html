Core Concepts

Intent becomes architecture.

FounderSignal treats a business idea as a source object. The validation engine checks market feasibility, the compiler emits technical artifacts, and the correction loop mutates the output when practitioner feedback reveals a constraint.

Regression-aware correction

Corrections are not comments. They become checks that must be reflected in spec, schema, tasks, and manifest outputs.
